Olympic Games

Swimming

An iconic sport in the Olympics, featuring a variety of styles like freestyle, butterfly, and backstroke, requiring speed and endurance.

Gymnastics

A display of strength, flexibility, and artistry, gymnastics includes events like floor routines and balance beam.

Rowing

A sport of coordination and power, rowing involves teams or individuals racing boats over a set distance on water.

Basketball

Teamwork and skill come together in this exciting sport, where nations compete for the gold medal on the court.

Tennis

Players showcase agility and power in singles and doubles matches, making tennis a popular Olympic event.
